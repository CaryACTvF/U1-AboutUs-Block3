Biography

I have done two internships thus far that are in the field of filmmaking and want to continue doing so. I am looking to major in Film Produciton in college as well.

I don't have many hobbies, I sleep when I can. However, when I'm not at school or sleeping (or both) I am watching Netflix or I'm with family. I have a dog named Blake, he is an idiot but I love him. I have 2 brothers, a twin brother named Aidan (a lot of people find it cool that my name is his name spelled backwards and vice versa), he is two minutes older than me, and Rafael who is 1 year older than me.

About Me

Biography:

I go to the Academy for Careers in Television and Film Highschool. I have two long lasting internships. One is at NYU Langone Hospital, where I film and edit short informational videos about sex, drugs and mental health. The other is at AfrogistMedia Inc., where I edit and occassionally film videos about the news.

I live in Brooklyn, NY. I'm a Scorpio. I have a cat named Maxine. I am currently in the post production stages of my independent project, "I Am The End," a political film about the climate crisis, generational differences, the LGBTQ community and the meaning of life.

This year, I will also be working on many new projects with my friend and creative collaborator, Trey Petgrave.

Technology:

I own a phone, and a computer, which I use to edit. I edit on Premiere Pro, Final Cut and Imovie when I have no alternative.
